### Cloud Computing : 

- Compute Power : meaning servers such Windows, Linux, hosting environments etc..
- Storage : like files and/or databases
- Networking : in Azure but also outside when connecting to your company network 
- Analytics : services for visualization and telemtry data

### Key Concepts : 

- Scalability : is the ability to scale, so allocate and deallocate ressources at any time.
- Elasticity : is the ability to scale dynamically
- Agility : is the ability to scale fast (scale quickly)
- Fault Tolerance : is the ability to maintain the system uptime while physical and service component failure happen
- Disaster Recovery : is the process and design principle which allows a system to recovers from natural or human induced disaster
- High Availability : is the agreed level of operational uptime for the system. It is a simple calcualtion of system uptime vs whole lifetime of the system
(availability = uptime/(uptime + downtime))
